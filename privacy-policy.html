Privacy Policy

1. Introduction & Overview

ColidbuMCP ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent relationship with our users and clients. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our websites, mobile applications, and services (collectively, the "Services").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services. By accessing and using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in various ways, including:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Registration: Name, email address, password, profile information
  • Contact Forms: Name, email, phone number, company name, message content
  • Service Requests: Project details, requirements, preferences, files
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Communications: Support tickets, emails, chat messages, feedback

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, interactions, session duration
  • Location Data: IP address, general geographic location (country/city level)
  • Cookies & Tracking: Cookies, pixels, web beacons, similar technologies
  • Analytics: User behavior patterns, conversion tracking, performance metrics

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Social Media: If you link your account to social platforms
  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics, Mixpanel, similar services
  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • Ad Networks: Google Ads, Facebook Ads, other advertising platforms
  • Business Partners: Referral information, partnership data

2.4 Information from Our Mobile Apps

  • App Store Data: App Store ID, device ID, advertising ID (IDFA/Google Advertising ID)
  • Push Notifications: Opt-in status, notification preferences
  • Crash Reports: App performance data, error logs
  • In-App Analytics: Feature usage, user journey, session data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Providing, maintaining, and improving our Services
  • Communication: Responding to inquiries, support requests, updates
  • Account Management: Creating and managing user accounts
  • Payment Processing: Processing transactions and billing
  • Personalization: Customizing user experience, recommendations

4.2 Types of Ads Displayed

Our applications may display the following types of advertisements:

  • Banner Ads: Static or animated ads displayed at the top or bottom of screens
  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen ads appearing between app content transitions
  • Rewarded Video Ads: Video advertisements users choose to watch for in-game rewards
  • Native Ads: Ad content styled to match the app's native design
  • Playable Ads: Interactive ads allowing users to engage before installation
  • Splash Ads: Full-screen ads displayed on app launch

4.3 Data Sharing with Ad Partners

When you use our apps, certain data may be automatically shared with advertising partners to enable ad serving and measurement. This includes:

  • Your Advertising ID (GAID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
  • Your Device ID and device information
  • Your approximate location (IP-based, not GPS)
  • App usage and engagement metrics
  • Inferred interests and demographic data

4.4 User Rights & Control

Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising:

  • iOS Users: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Turn off "Personalized Ads"
  • Android Users: Settings > Google > Manage Your Google Account > Data & Privacy > Ad Personalization Settings
  • Limit Ad Tracking (iOS): Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Enable "Limit Ad Tracking"
  • Opt Out of Ads Personalization (Android): Use Google's Ads Preferences tool

Reset Your Advertising ID:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Reset Advertising ID
  • Android: Settings > Google > Manage Your Google Account > Data & Privacy > Reset Advertising ID

10. Regional Privacy Rights & Compliance

10.1 GDPR Rights (EU, EEA, UK)

If you are located in the EU, EEA, or UK, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
  • Right to Restriction: Restrict processing of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing for direct marketing or profiling
  • Rights Related to Automated Decision Making: Opt-out of automated decisions
  • Right to Lodge Complaints: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

10.2 CCPA/CPRA Rights (California)

If you are a California resident, you have these rights under CCPA/CPRA:

  • Right to Know: Request what personal information we collect, use, and share
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information (with exceptions)
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of "sales" or "sharing" of personal information
  • Right to Limit: Limit use of sensitive personal information
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Opt-Out of Profiling: Opt-out of automated decision-making
  • Non-Discrimination: No discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

10.3 LGPD Rights (Brazil)

If you are in Brazil, LGPD grants you these rights:

  • Right to Access: Access your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct incomplete or inaccurate data
  • Right to Deletion: Request data deletion
  • Right to Data Portability: Obtain your data in portable format
  • Right to Information: Know how your data is processed
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing

10.4 PIPEDA Rights (Canada)

Under Canada's PIPEDA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • File complaints with Canada's Privacy Commissioner
  • Withdraw consent at any time

10.5 Other Regional Rights

Users in other countries may have additional privacy rights. We respect all applicable regional privacy laws including APEC Cross-Border Privacy Rules and various national data protection laws.
