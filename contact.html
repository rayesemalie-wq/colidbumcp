Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with ColidbuMCP?

Simply contact us via email or fill out the contact form. Our team will review your inquiry and schedule a consultation to discuss your project requirements and timeline.

What platforms do you support?

We support iOS (App Store), Android (Google Play), and other major mobile platforms. We can also assist with cross-platform development and optimization.

How long does game development take?

Development timeline depends on project scope and complexity. A typical casual game project can take 3-6 months, though this varies. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you provide publishing support?

Yes! We offer complete publishing services including App Store and Google Play submission, optimization, and ongoing support for maximum visibility.

What are your pricing models?

We offer flexible service packages including fixed-price projects, time-and-materials, and revenue-sharing models. Pricing is customized based on your specific needs.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Absolutely. We offer 24/7 technical support, regular updates, bug fixes, and continuous optimization to keep your games performing excellently.

Can you help with game monetization?

Yes. We specialize in monetization strategy, ad network integration (AdMob, AppLovin, etc.), and in-app purchase optimization to maximize your revenue.

Do you support multiple languages?

Yes. We provide localization services for 50+ languages and regions, ensuring your games reach global audiences effectively while maintaining cultural relevance.
